About Juanet

Juanet exists to provide reliable power and internet services to low-income earners — putting dependable energy and connectivity within reach of communities that need it most.

What we offer

Manufactured power units sized to fit different households and needs, paired with internet and airtime services delivered through the same app — plus maintenance tracking so every device keeps performing at its best.
